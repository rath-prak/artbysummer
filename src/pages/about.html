Hello! I'm Rath, a digital designer based in Wellington New Zealand. I'm a passionate problem solver and have been working in the creative industry since 2009, firstly as a visual designer and now as a digital designer with a focus on UI/UX.

Prototyping, looking at colours, creating intuitive interfaces and exploring new design systems are some of the things I enjoy most.

I have a passion for new and emerging technologies and have a particular interest in conversational user interfaces, AI, social tech, blockchain technology and cryptoeconomics. I believe blockchain technology not only has the potential to redefine the way we live but also offers new and exciting challenges for designers.

When I'm not a designer, I like to dabble in photography, talk about movies and watch a rugby game.
